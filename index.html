Provider Policies
Read and accept before joining

⚙️ Service Standards

Arrive within the scheduled time. Late arrivals must notify the customer at least 15 minutes in advance.
Wear the FixIt-provided uniform or ID badge during every job.
Maintain professional behaviour and use polite language at all times.
Do not accept cash payments outside the app. All payments must be processed through FixIt.
Carry your own tools. FixIt is not responsible for missing equipment.

💳 Payment & Earnings Policy

FixIt retains a 15% platform commission on every booking. Your net earnings are credited after deduction.
Weekly payouts are processed every Monday for the previous week's completed jobs.
Disputed or cancelled bookings (customer fault) will be credited within 3–5 working days.
TDS at applicable rates will be deducted as per the Income Tax Act. Annual Form 16A will be provided.
Fraudulent transactions will result in immediate account suspension and forfeiture of pending earnings.

⭐ Ratings & Performance

Maintain a minimum rating of 3.5 stars to remain active on the platform.
Providers with ratings below 3.0 for 3 consecutive weeks will be temporarily suspended.
Job cancellation rate must be below 10%. Excessive cancellations reduce your job allocation priority.
Accepting a job and not showing up (no-show) is a serious violation and may lead to permanent deactivation.

🚫 Code of Conduct

Zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, or inappropriate behaviour towards customers.
Do not solicit direct contact or bookings outside the FixIt platform.
Providers must not share customer personal data with any third party.
Any damage caused to customer property during a job must be reported immediately via the app.
FixIt reserves the right to suspend or permanently deactivate accounts violating these policies.

🔒 Privacy & Data

Your Aadhaar, PAN, and bank details are encrypted and used only for KYC and payments.
Location data is shared with customers only during an active booking.
FixIt will not sell your personal data to third parties.
